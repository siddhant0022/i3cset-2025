For Authors

Authors are encouraged to contribute research papers, review articles, and case studies that highlight recent innovations, practical implementations, or theoretical breakthroughs in the following thematic areas:

Track 1: Communication Systems and 6G Technologies

Communication Protocols and 6G, Wireless and Mobile Communications & Technologies, Communication Theory, Digital Communication, Satellite Communication, Telecommunication and Networking, Communication Systems, Telecommunication Technologies, Green Communication, Environment-sustainable Communications.

Track 2: Signal, Image & Multimedia Processing

DSP Algorithms and Architectures, Digital Signal Processing, Signal and Image Processing, Image Processing, Multimedia Processing, Multimedia Engineering.

Track 3: Embedded Systems, IoT & Cyber-Physical Networks

IoT and Embedded Systems, Embedded Systems, Wireless Sensor Networks, Ad hoc Networks, Robotics, Control Systems and Theory.

Track 4: Antennas, Propagation & Microwave Technologies

Antenna and Propagation, Microwave Communication, Optical Communications, Opto Electronics and Optical Communication, Photonics.

Track 5: Cybersecurity, Blockchain & Information Technologies

Cybersecurity and Blockchain Technologies, Network Security, Information Theory, Information Theory and Coding Theory, Information Technologies and Applications, Information Technology Application, Data Communications.

Track 6: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Data Science

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Pattern Analysis and Classification, Pattern Recognition and Image Processing, Big Data Analytics and Data Sciences, Optimization Techniques, Software and Database Systems, Bioinformatics and Scientific Computing.

Track 7: Electronic Design, VLSI & Digital Circuits

Circuit and System, Discrete Electronics Circuits, VLSI Design, Computer Architecture and Systems.

Track 8: Cloud, Quantum & Advanced Computing Paradigms

Cloud, Edge and Quantum Computing, Engineering Education, Engineering Management, Social Implications of Technology.

Track 9: Sustainable Computing, Smart Energy & Green Technologies

Sustainable Computing and Green IT, Power and Energy, Smart Grids and Renewable Energy Systems, Disasters and Humanitarian Technology.

Track 10: Biomedical Systems, Bioelectronics & Oceanic Engineering

Biomedical Engineering, Bioelectronics and Biosensors, Marine and Offshore Engineering.

Track 11: Miscellaneous and Interdisciplinary Topics

Social Implications of Technology, Engineering Education, Engineering Management, Emerging Interdisciplinary Applications, Multidisciplinary Research Integration, Technological Innovation for Society, Applications in Agriculture, Disaster Mitigation Technologies.
