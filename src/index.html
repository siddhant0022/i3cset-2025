ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The International Conference on Computing, Communication, and Sustainable Energy Technologies (I3CSET-2025) is a major international conference that brings together top researchers, scientists, engineers, and scholars from across the world to share their latest studies and experiences in Communication, Computing, and Energy Efficiency. The conference, which includes keynote speakers, technical sessions, and workshops, covers a wide range of contemporary areas. Topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to following thrust areas:

Communication, Networking, Circuits & Components, Devices and Systems, Computing and Processing Field, Waves and Electromagnetics, Photonics and Electro-optics, Electrical Power, and Energy & Industry Applications.
